Income groups, and distribution by gender

Based on a sample dataset of "Census Income" collected in 1994, we explore how gender is distributed across income groups. The groups are classified by whether income is greater than or less than $50K/yr.

Data used from Adult dataset.

Gender Occupation Marital status Age vs Hours per week
Men do well in sales and women do well in Other services

In the >50k Income group men tend to do 5 times better in sales with a ratio of 5.38. And the best occupation for women in the >50k Income group is Other-services with a ratio of 2.03.

In the <50k Income group women tend to do 2 times worse than men with a ratio of 0.55. And men do the worst in Private-housing-services with a Ratio of 1.01.
