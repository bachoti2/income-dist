Income groups, and distribution by gender

Based on a sample dataset of "Census Income" collected in 1994, we explore how gender is distributed across income groups. The groups are classified by whether income is greater than or less than $50K/yr.

Data used from Adult dataset.

Divorced and Widowed women not doing well in Tech support

In the >50k Income group, in Tech-support, Divorced men tend to do 7 times better than Divorced women with a Ratio of 6.64. And Widowed men tend to do 8.5 times better than Widowed women. Transport-moving has a similar trend.
